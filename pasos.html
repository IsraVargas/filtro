How to make a water filter?



MATERIALS

  • 1 plastic container with cylinder shape and funnel termination, approximately 1/2m height, in another case, an empty bottle
  • 1Kg fine sand
  • 1kg gravel
  • 1kg of small rocks
  • 1kg of medium rocks
  • 1/2 kg of activated charcoal

4.The container is used with the funnel termination below. It’s made small perforations below of the container to allow the filtered water to come out.

5.The container is cleaned with water and soap. 6.When the container is cleaned, must be collocated in the base that allows outlet water.
  • 1. Small rocks 10cm
  • 2. Medium rocks 15cm
  • 3. Gravel 3cm
  • 4. Charcoal 3cm
  • 5. Gravel 2cm
  • 6. Fine sand 5cm
  • 7. Gravel 5cm
  • 8. Small rocks 10cm
7. Finally, the water is poured inside of the filter to check that it's work
